How to play yatzy

In the list below you will be able to see same of the major rules for playing yatzy. A more detailed guide can be found here.

The game is played with 5 dice with a scoreboard with different combos. The goal of the game is to get the highest score possible. The first six rounds you can gain points in the following list. During your turn you throw your five dice once and choose in which of the six categories that you want points.

  • Ones
  • Twos
  • Threes
  • Fours
  • Fives
  • Sixes

After the first six rounds if you have 63 or more points after the first half you'll gain a bonus of fifty. Now the second half starts and thus we have now combos.

  • One pair
  • Two pairs
  • Three of a kind
  • Four of a kind
  • Small straight
  • Large straight
  • Full house
  • Chance
  • Yatzy
